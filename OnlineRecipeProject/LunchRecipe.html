Tuna Salad Sandwich with Lemon Dill Recipe

Tuna Salad Sandwich with Lemon Dill is a light and flavorful sandwich with flaked tuna, chopped vegetables, and a refreshing lemon dill dressing

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine flaked tuna, mayonnaise, celery, red onion, dill, and lemon juice.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Spread the tuna salad mixture on one slice of bread.
  4. Top with the other slice of bread.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Whole-Wheat Bread
  • 1 can (5 oz) Tuna in Water, drained
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  • 41 tablespoon Chopped Celery
  • 1 tablespoon Chopped Red Onion
  • 1 teaspoon Fresh Dill, chopped (or 1/2 teaspoon dried dill)
  • 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Leftover Veggie Power Bowl with Tahini Dressing Recipe

Leftover Veggie Power Bowl with Tahini Dressing is a customizable lunch bowl with roasted veggies, greens, and a creamy tahini sauce.

Procedure:

  1. If using leftover roasted vegetables, reheat them in a microwave or oven.
  2. In a bowl, combine cooked quinoa or brown rice, roasted vegetables, chopped greens, cucumber, and cherry tomatoes.
  3. Drizzle with tahini dressing and toss to coat.
  4. Top with your favorite toppings like crumbled feta cheese, avocado slices, or chopped nuts (optional).

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cooked Quinoa or Brown Rice
  • 1/2 cup Roasted Vegetables (broccoli, carrots, chickpeas, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Greens (spinach, kale, lettuce)
  • 1/4 cup Sliced Cucumber
  • 1/4 cup Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons Tahini Dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • Optional toppings: crumbled feta cheese, avocado slices, chopped nuts

Leftover Chicken Caesar Wrap Recipe

Leftover Chicken Caesar Wrap is a quick and easy wrap made with leftover chicken, romaine lettuce, parmesan cheese, and Caesar dressing.

Procedure:

  1. Spread Caesar salad dressing evenly over the tortilla.
  2. Layer with chopped romaine lettuce, shredded chicken, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, starting from one long side.
  4. Cut the wrap in half and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Flour Tortilla
  • 1 cup Shredded Cooked Chicken
  • 1/2 cup Romaine Lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Caesar Salad Dressing

Mediterranean Chickpea Salad Pita Bread Recipe

Mediterranean Chickpea Salad Pita Bread is a protein-packed pita filled with a flavorful chickpea salad made with cucumber, red onion, bell pepper, feta cheese, and olives.

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, red onion, bell pepper, feta cheese, and kalamata olives.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, and oregano.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chickpea mixture and toss to coat.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Stuff the chickpea salad mixture into warmed pita breads and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 11 can (15 oz) Chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Cucumber
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Red Onion
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Bell Pepper (any color)
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Feta Cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Chopped Kalamata Olives
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 whole Pita Breads, warmed

Caprese Panini with Pesto Recipe

Caprese Panini with Pesto is a warm and cheesy pressed sandwich with pesto, fresh mozzarella, tomato, and basil.

Procedure:

  1. Brush both slices of sourdough bread with olive oil.
  2. Spread pesto on one slice of bread.
  3. Layer with sliced mozzarella, tomato, and fresh basil leaves.
  4. Top with the other slice of bread.
  5. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium heat.
  6. Grill the panini for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  7. Cut the panini in half and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Sourdough Bread
  • 1 tablespoon Pesto
  • 1 Fresh Mozzarella Ball, sliced
  • 1 Tomato, sliced
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Olive Oil (for brushing)

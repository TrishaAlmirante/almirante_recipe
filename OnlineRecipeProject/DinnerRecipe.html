One-Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken with Roasted Vegetables Recipe

One-Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken with Roasted Vegetables is a tender chicken and colorful veggies roasted together with a bright lemon garlic flavor.

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, toss chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Arrange chicken breasts in a single layer in a baking dish.
  4. Scatter lemon slices, broccoli florets, and bell pepper slices around the chicken.
  5. Pour chicken broth into the bottom of the pan.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
  7. Broil for an additional 1-2 minutes, if desired, for crispier vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 head Broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 red Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Chicken Broth

Creamy Tomato Pasta with Spinach and Sun-dried Tomatoes Recipe

Creamy Tomato Pasta with Spinach and Sun-dried Tomatoes is acomforting pasta dish with a creamy tomato sauce, wilted spinach, and tangy sun-dried tomatoes.

Procedure:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, sun-dried tomatoes (if using), and a pinch of red pepper flakes (optional).
  6. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and add it to the sauce along with fresh spinach.
  9. Toss everything together until spinach is wilted and sauce is heated through.
  10. Serve immediately with additional Parmesan cheese for sprinkling, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Pasta (penne, rotini, or your favorite)
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 cup Sun-dried Tomatoes, chopped (optional)
  • 1 cup Fresh Spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated (plus extra for serving)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Honey Garlic Salmon with Roasted Asparagus Recipe

Honey Garlic Salmon with Roasted Asparagus is a simple and flavorful dish with glazed salmon and roasted asparagus.

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, soy sauce, honey, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and black.
  3. Place salmon fillets in a baking dish and brush with half of the marinade.
  4. Toss asparagus with a bit of olive oil and salt and pepper. Arrange asparagus on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until cooked through and flaky.
  6. Bake the asparagus for the last 5 minutes of cooking time for the salmon.
  7. Brush the salmon with the remaining marinade during the last few minutes of baking for a glossy finish (optional).

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Honey
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1 bunch Asparagus, trimmed

Cheesy Chicken Enchiladas with Green Chili Sauce Recipe

Cheesy Chicken Enchiladas with Green Chili Sauce is a Tex-Mex classic with shredded chicken, green chili sauce, and melty cheese wrapped in tortillas.

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, green chili sauce, cheddar cheese, and mozzarella cheese.
  3. If using, heat olive oil in a pan and saute chopped onion and garlic until softened. Add to the chicken mixture.
  4. Spread a thin layer of green chili sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Warm corn tortillas in a microwave or skillet to make them pliable.
  6. Fill each tortilla with a scoop of the chicken mixture and roll them up.
  7. Place the rolled enchiladas seam-side down in the baking dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Shredded Cooked Chicken
  • 1 (10 oz) can Green Chili Sauce
  • 1 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 6 Corn Tortillas
  • 1 can (15 oz) Black Beans, rinsed and drained (optional)
  • 1 cup Chopped Onion (optional)
  • 1 clove Garlic, minced (optional)
  • Olive Oil (for cooking)

Cheesy Chicken Fajitas Recipe

Cheesy Chicken Fajitas is a customizable meal with seasoned chicken and colorful peppers, all wrapped in warm tortillas and melted cheese.

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced bell peppers and onion. Sauté for 5-7 minutes, or until softened and slightly browned.
  3. Add the chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and garlic powder to the pan. Stir to coat the vegetables and cook for an additional minute, allowing the spices to release their aroma.
  4. Add the shredded cooked chicken to the pan. If using leftover chicken, heat it through with the vegetables. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. While the chicken and vegetables are heating, warm the tortillas according to package instructions (microwave, stovetop, or using a tortilla warmer)
  6. Divide the chicken and vegetable mixture evenly among the warmed tortillas.
  7. Sprinkle each tortilla with shredded cheese.
  8. (Optional) To melt the cheese further, place the filled tortillas on a baking sheet and broil for 1-2 minutes, watching closely to avoid burning.
  9. Serve immediately with your favorite toppings like chopped avocado, salsa, sour cream, lime wedges, and chopped fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 pound Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts (cooked and shredded, or leftover)
  • 1 large Red Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 1 large Green Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium Onion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon Chili Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese (or your favorite melting cheese)
  • 4 large Flour Tortillas
  • Optional toppings: Chopped avocado, salsa, sour cream, lime wedges, chopped fresh cilantro

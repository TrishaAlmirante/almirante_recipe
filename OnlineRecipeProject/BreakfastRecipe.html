Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Cheese Recipe

Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Cheese is a savory breakfast with fluffy eggs, wilted spinach, and melted cheese.

Procedure:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk them together with a fork until well beaten.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped spinach to the pan and cook until wilted, about 1 minute.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan with the spinach.
  5. As the eggs begin to set, gently stir them with a spatula, breaking up large curds.
  6. Once the eggs are almost cooked through, add the shredded cheese.
  7. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are fully set and the cheese is melted.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve immediately with toast or whole-wheat bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Overnight Oats with Berries and Chia Seeds Recipe

Overnight Oats with Berries and Chia Seeds is a no-cook breakfast with creamy oats, fresh berries, and crunchy chia seeds.

Procedure:

  1. In a jar or container, combine rolled oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, and any additional flavorings like honey and cinnamon.
  2. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
  3. Cover the jar and refrigerate overnight.
  4. In the morning, stir the oats again and top with fresh berries for extra flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Rolled Oats
  • 1 cup Milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/4 cup Yogurt (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 tablespoon Chia Seeds
  • 1/4 cup Berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon Honey (optional)
  • Pinch of cinnamon (optional)

Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie Recipe

Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie is a refreshing and nutritious blend of yogurt, fruit, and milk for a quick and delicious start.

Procedure:

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add more milk or yogurt if needed to adjust the consistency.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Plain Yogurt (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 cup Frozen Fruit (bananas, berries, mango, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup Milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 tablespoon Honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla extract (optional)

Whole-Wheat Pancakes with Banana and Walnut Recipe

Whole-Wheat Pancakes with Banana and Walnut is a Heartier pancakes made with whole-wheat flour, mashed banana, and chopped walnuts.

Procedure:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and mashed banana.
  3. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients, stirring just until combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Fold in the chopped walnuts.
  5. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium heat.
  6. Pour batter onto the griddle for each pancake, leaving space between them.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Serve pancakes with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh fruit, or yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Whole-Wheat Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 ripe Banana, mashed
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Walnuts
  • 2 piece siling haba

Avocado Toast with Poached Egg Recipe

Avocado Toast with Poached Egg is a simple and satisfying open-faced toast with creamy avocado and a perfectly cooked poached egg.

Procedure:

  1. Toast a slice of whole-wheat bread.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the poached egg. Bring a pot of water to a simmer. Add vinegar to the water.
  3. Crack an egg into a small bowl. Create a whirlpool in the simmering water using a spoon. Gently swirl the water and then drop the egg into the center.
  4. Cook for 3-4 minutes, or until the egg white is set and the yolk is cooked to your preference.
  5. Spread mashed avocado on the toasted bread.
  6. Carefully remove the poached egg from the water with a slotted spoon and place it on top of the avocado toast.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Top with your favorite toppings like everything bagel seasoning, hot sauce, or red pepper flakes (optional).

Ingredients

  • 1 slice Whole-Wheat Toast
  • 1/2 ripe Avocado, mashed
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tablespoon Vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: Everything bagel seasoning, hot sauce, red pepper flakes
